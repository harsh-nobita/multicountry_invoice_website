1. Overview

Make Free Invoice ("we", "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we collect and use information when you use our website.

Make Free Invoice is designed with privacy first. Invoice data you enter is stored only in your browser's local storage and is never transmitted to our servers.

We may collect:

  • Anonymous usage analytics (page views, feature usage) via Google Analytics.
  • Information you voluntarily provide when contacting us.

3. Google AdSense

We use Google AdSense to display advertisements. Google may use cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits to our website or other websites. You can opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google's Ads Settings.

4. Cookies

We use cookies for analytics and advertising purposes. You can disable cookies in your browser settings.

5. Third-Party Services

Our site uses Google Fonts, Google Analytics, and Google AdSense. These services have their own privacy policies.

6. Your Rights

Since invoice data is stored locally on your device, you have full control. You can clear it at any time through your browser settings.

Questions? Contact us at privacy@make-free-invoice.com.
