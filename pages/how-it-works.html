How Make Free Invoice Works

Create a professional, tax-compliant invoice in under 2 minutes. No account needed.

1

Select Your Country & Currency

Choose your country from 150+ options. Make Free Invoice automatically sets the correct currency, date format, and applicable tax system (GST, VAT, HST, etc.).

2

Enter Your Business & Client Details

Click any field on the invoice to edit it directly. Add your company name, address, logo, and client billing details.

3

Add Line Items

Add your products or services with description, quantity, unit, and rate. The invoice calculates totals automatically.

4

Configure Tax

The tax system is pre-configured for your country. You can adjust rates, add multiple tax lines (e.g., CGST + SGST for India), or remove taxes entirely.

5

Download PDF

Click "Download PDF" to get a pixel-perfect, print-ready invoice in seconds. No watermarks. No limits.
