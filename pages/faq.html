Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our free invoice generator.

⚡ General

Is Make Free Invoice really free?

Yes — completely free. No sign-up required, no watermarks on your invoices, and no limits on the number of invoices you can create. We keep the service free through non-intrusive advertising.

Do I need to create an account?

No. Make Free Invoice works entirely in your browser with no login required. Your invoice data is saved locally on your device.

Is my invoice data stored on your servers?

No. All invoice data is stored in your browser's localStorage — it never leaves your device and is never sent to our servers. This means your financial data is completely private.

Which browsers are supported?

Make Free Invoice works on all modern browsers:

  • Google Chrome (recommended)
  • Mozilla Firefox
  • Microsoft Edge
  • Safari (macOS & iOS)
  • Opera

📄 Creating Invoices

How do I edit the invoice fields?

Simply click any text on the invoice to edit it directly. Fields like company name, address, client details, and notes are all editable in-place. Input fields (invoice number, dates, line item prices) are standard inputs you can type into.

Can I add my company logo?

Yes. Click the logo area in the top-left of the invoice to upload your logo image. Supported formats: PNG, JPG, SVG, WebP. For best results, use a logo with a transparent background (PNG).

How do I add more line items?

Click the "+ Add Line Item" button below the item table. You can add as many items as needed. Click the trash icon on any row to remove it.

Can I add a discount?

Yes. Click the "+ Discount" button in the options row below the item table. You can enter either a percentage discount (e.g., 10%) or a flat amount (e.g., $50). The total updates automatically.

How do I add shipping/delivery charges?

Click "+ Shipping" in the options row. Enter the shipping amount and it will be added to the invoice total.

Can I change the invoice language?

Yes! The Language selector in the settings bar lets you switch all invoice labels to:

  • English, Spanish, French, German, Portuguese, Arabic, Chinese, Japanese, Hindi

Arabic automatically switches the invoice to right-to-left (RTL) layout.

How do I change the invoice color / branding?

Use the color swatches in the settings bar to choose a theme color. This changes the invoice header, title, and accent colors. Choose from navy, green, purple, red, amber, or black.

🌍 Countries, Currencies & Taxes

How do I set the country and currency?

Use the Country dropdown in the settings bar. Selecting a country automatically sets the correct currency, date format, and tax system. You can also change the currency independently using the Currency dropdown.

Which tax systems are supported?

Make Free Invoice supports all major international tax systems:

  • GST – India (CGST+SGST/IGST), Australia, New Zealand, Singapore, Canada, Pakistan
  • VAT – EU countries, UK, UAE, Saudi Arabia, South Africa, Russia
  • HST/PST/QST – Canadian provinces
  • MwSt – Germany, Austria, Switzerland
  • TVA – France
  • IVA – Italy, Spain, Mexico, Argentina, Colombia
  • SST – Malaysia
  • PPN – Indonesia
  • JCT – Japan (消費税)
  • Sales Tax – United States (by state)
Can I add multiple tax lines (e.g., CGST + SGST)?

Yes. When you select India, Make Free Invoice automatically adds CGST and SGST as separate tax lines. For any country, you can click "+ Add Tax" to add additional tax rows with custom names and rates.

How do I enter my GST/VAT registration number?

When you select a country with a tax registration requirement, a registration field appears below the billing address section. Enter your GSTIN, ABN, VAT number, or other tax ID there.

💾 Saving & Downloading

How do I download the invoice as a PDF?

Click the blue "Download PDF" button in the header or action bar. The invoice will be generated and downloaded as a PDF file automatically. No server is involved — the PDF is generated in your browser.

How do I save my invoice to continue later?

Click the "Save" button in the action bar. Your invoice is saved to your browser's local storage. Click "Load Invoice" to restore it. Note: clearing your browser data will erase saved invoices.

Is there auto-save?

Yes. Make Free Invoice auto-saves your work every 10 seconds to the browser's local storage. If you close the tab and return, your invoice should be restored automatically.

Can I print the invoice instead of downloading PDF?

Yes. Click the "Print" button in the action bar or press Ctrl+P (Cmd+P on Mac). The invoice is optimized for printing with a dedicated print stylesheet — all buttons and ads are hidden in print view.

Can I send the invoice by email?

Click the "Send Email" button to open your default email client with the invoice subject pre-filled. You'll need to attach the downloaded PDF manually. For direct email delivery, consider hosting your own version with an email API integration.

⚙️ Technical

Can I self-host Make Free Invoice?

Yes. Make Free Invoice is a pure HTML/CSS/JavaScript application with no backend. Simply drop the files on any web server or open index.html directly in a browser. See the README.md for full setup instructions.

Does it work offline?

Mostly yes. The core invoice editor works offline. PDF generation requires loading a JavaScript library from a CDN on first use (it's cached after that). Google Fonts and ads require internet access.

Can I add more currencies or tax rates?

Yes — just edit js/countries.js (for currencies and country data) and js/taxsystems.js (for tax system definitions). Both files are well-commented and easy to extend.
