Frequently Asked Questions

Is Make Free Invoice really free with no watermarks?

Yes — completely free, forever. No account required, no watermarks on your PDF invoices, and no limit on the number of invoices you can create. The service is supported by non-intrusive advertising.

Which tax systems are supported?

GST (India, Australia, Canada, New Zealand, Singapore, Pakistan), VAT (EU, UK, UAE, Saudi Arabia, South Africa, Russia), HST/PST/QST (Canada provinces), MwSt (Germany, Austria, Switzerland), TVA (France), IVA (Italy, Spain, Mexico, Argentina), SST (Malaysia), PPN (Indonesia), JCT — Japan Consumption Tax, US Sales Tax by state, and many more.

How does the country auto-detection (GEO) work?

When the page loads, Make Free Invoice uses your browser's timezone and language settings to estimate your country. It then pre-selects the correct currency and tax system. You can always change it manually using the Country dropdown.

Can I add multiple tax lines (e.g., CGST + SGST in India)?

Yes. Make Free Invoice automatically adds CGST and SGST as separate lines when you select India. You can also click "+ Add Tax" to add any number of custom tax lines with your own names and rates.

Is my invoice data safe and private?

Completely. All invoice data is stored only in your browser's local storage — it never leaves your device and is never sent to any server. Clearing browser data will erase it.

Can I save my invoice and come back later?

Yes. Make Free Invoice auto-saves every 10 seconds to local storage. You can also click "Save" manually and "Load Invoice" to restore it in any future session.
